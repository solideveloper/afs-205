How Does Soli.nyc's Assistance Work?

Most help is done via Zoom video conference. You will receive an email or text-message 1 hour prior to your appointment time with all the information you will need for us to connect. All conferences are recorded for quality as well as your reference upon purchase. This means you do not have to worry about missing important steps or having to purchase another appointment slot for the same service. You will be able to reference your video conference with Soli.nyc over and over, Anytime.
